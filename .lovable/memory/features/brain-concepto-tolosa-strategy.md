## Brand Identity: Brain Concepto To.Lo.Sa.
- **Tagline**: "Tu casa cuida de ti"
- **Tone**: Cálido + Orgánico/Natural + Emocional
- **Target**: Eco-conscientes (all ages, sustainability & health first)
- **Structure**: Landing storytelling (public) → App interna (after login)

## Core Business Strategy
- **Heart**: Single-family Passivhaus/passive homes
- **Key message**: Health & wellbeing (air quality, thermal comfort, humidity control)
- **Standards**: Passivhaus certification, A+++ energy rating, 90% energy savings

## Design System (v3.0)
- **Primary**: Forest green (HSL 152 45% 38%) — nature, health, trust
- **Accent**: Warm terracotta (HSL 25 80% 52%) — warmth, earth, emotion
- **Background**: Warm cream (HSL 30 25% 96%)
- **Fonts**: Plus Jakarta Sans (body) + Playfair Display (display/italic accents)
